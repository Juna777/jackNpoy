How to Play the Rock Paper Scissors Game:

1.The game interface consists of three images representing the options: rock, paper, and scissors.
2.To make your choice, click on one of the images: "rock", "paper", or "scissors".
3.After you make your choice, the computer will randomly select its choice.
4. The result of the round will be displayed below the options. It will indicate whether you won, lost, or tied with the computer, and it will also
show the current scores.
5.A history of previous rounds will be displayed below the result. It will show the choices and results of each round.
6.The game will continue for a total of 5 rounds.
7.After the 5th round, the final result will be displayed below the history. It will indicate whether you won the game, lost the game, or if it was
a tie.
8.To start a new game, click on the "New Game" button.
9.The scores, history, and result will be reset, and you can start playing again.
10.Remember, rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.
